About Us

Welcome to Miftah, an innovative school initiative. Our mission is to provide high-quality, holistic education that fosters intellectual curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. We are a passionate team of individuals dedicated to creating a transformative learning environment. We dedicated ourselves to improving the quality of education in India, filling every gap that exists in the current Education System by building an ideal school.
